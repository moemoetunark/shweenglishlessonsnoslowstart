Passive Voice Future Perfect Examples

အနာဂတ်အချိန်ကါလ တစ်ခုမှာ action တစ်ခုဟာ ပြီးလိမ့်မည်၊ပြီးဆုံးလိမ့်မည်ဆိုတဲ့ အဓိပ္ပါည်ဖြင့် passive voice future perfect tense ကိုသုံးပါတယ်၊

ဝါကျတည်ဆောက်ပုံကတော့

S + will have been + v3. ဖြစ်ပါတယ်

Example Sentences: နမူနာဝါကျများ

Sentence 1 (Active Voice): They will have finished the project by the end of this month.ဒီလအကုန်မှာသူတို့ ပရောဂျက် ပြီးဆုံးလိမ့်မည်

Passive Voice: The project will have been finished by them by the end of this month.

Sentence 2 (Active Voice): She will have written the report before the meeting.အစည်းဝေးပွဲမစတင်မှီသူမ အစီရင်ခံစာ ရေးပြီးလိမ့်မည်

Passive Voice: The report will have been written by her before the meeting.အဓိပ္ပါယ်တူ

Sentence 3 (Active Voice): We will have completed the training by the time you arrive.မင်းရောက်တဲ့အခါ ငါတို့လေ့ကျင့်ခန်း ပြီးဆုံးလိမ့်မည်

Passive Voice: The training will have been completed by us by the time you arrive. ။

Sentence 4 (Active Voice): He will have repaired the car by next week.နောက်အပတ်ဆိုသူကားကို ပြပြင် ပြီးလိမ့်မယ်

Passive Voice: The car will have been repaired by him by next week. ။

Sentence 5 (Active Voice): They will have cooked dinner by the time the guests arrive.အည့်သည်တွေရောက်တဲ့အခါ သူတို့ညစာချက်ပြီးလိမ့်မည်

Passive Voice: Dinner will have been cooked by them by the time the guests arrive. ။
